Here's some cool stuff from 2013.

  • Ma'ono Fried Chicken & Whiskey

    Pete

    I went to dinner here with friends on my birthday last year. Their Hawaiian fried chicken is unique, high quality, and not at all pretentious. If you're ever looking for a great meal in Seattle, I recommend it.

  • Tedeschi Trucks Band Made Up Mind

    Pete

    The latest album from one of my favorite bands. Their modern blues features some tremendous vocal and instrumental talent. They also put on great live shows (though I was unable to catch them in 2013).

  • A Death in Brazil by Peter Robb

    Pete

    this is probably the coolest history book I've ever read. Covers both the early colonial history and more recent political drama and reads like a novel. Fascinating stuff.

  • Sulphur Mountain Cosmic Ray Station

    Pete

    Sulphur Mountain Cosmic Ray Station in Banff, Canada. My friends and I hiked to the top and took a bunch of great photos. Scenery shots are pretty easy when you can find a great view like the top of a mountain but I like this one because it's not simply looking straight down.

  • David Simon: Trayvon

    Pete

    David Simon's blog is full of great writing about a lot of different topics but I particularly enjoyed this piece. It's brief but still fully encompasses my frustration with the death of Trayvon and the acquittal of his murderer.

  • Shantaram

    John

    An excellent book about adventure, crime, and the human experience. The novel is based off of the author's life as a fugitive and mafioso in Bombay, India.

  • Smokedaddy

    John

    Excellent BBQ, beer, and live music. The first time we ate there we went back three more times that week! Damn, thats some tasty pig.

  • The Avett Brothers

    John

    Saw them again with Chance in November. Brings total Avett shows to 10.

  • Cancun

    John

    Crazy ass party times with the Meyers clan. Discovered Caipirinhas, a delicious drink of white rum, sugar, and limes. I could drink 'em for breakfast.

  • Wicker Park

    John

    Been in the neighborhood for a year and a half now. Great bar and restaurant scene and the rent aint so bad.
